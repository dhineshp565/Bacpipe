tet(R)	"does not confer resistance to tetracycline class antimicrobials directly, but instead regulates the expression of tetH; often detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in a resistance region beside tetH, along with the msrE-mphE operon"
tet(Y)	"confers resistance to tetracycline class antimicrobials (Category III) including oxytetracycline and chlortetracycline; recently detected on an integrative and conjugative element (ICE) harboured by an M. haemolytica isolate in Europe, not previously detected in North America"

mphE	"confers resistance to macrolide class antimicrobials (Category II) including tulathromycin, gamithromycin, tilmicosin, tildipirosin and tylosin; most frequently detected as part of an operon-like structure (i.e., a cluster of linked genes) with msrE; often detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in a resistance region with tetH"
erm(42)	"confers resistance to macrolide class antimicrobials (Category II) including tulathromycin, gamithromycin, tilmicosin, tildipirosin and tylosin; sometimes detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in a resistance region with APH(3')-Ia, APH(3'')-Ib, aph(6)-Id, and sul2"
estT	"confers resistance to 16-membered ring-containing macrolide subclass antimicrobials (Category II) including tilmicosin, tildipirosin and tylosin; recently detected in some strains of BRD-associated bacteria near APH(3')-Ia, APH(3'')-Ib, aph(6)-Id, and sul2, when those genes were also present"
mef(C)	"confers resistance to macrolide class antimicrobials (Category II) including tulathromycin and/or tilmicosin; recently detected on an integrative and conjugative element (ICE) harboured by an M. haemolytica isolate in Europe, not previously detected in North America"
mphG	"confers resistance to macrolide class antimicrobials (Category II) including tulathromycin and/or tilmicosin; recently detected on an integrative and conjugative element (ICE) harboured by an M. haemolytica isolate in Europe, not previously detected in North America"
sul2	"confers resistance to sulfonamide class antimicrobials (Category III), including sulfamethazine and sulfadoxine (used in combination with trimethoprim in feedlot production medicine); often detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in complete linkage with APH(3')-Ia, APH(3'')-Ib, and aph(6)-Id"

APH(3'')-Ib	"sometimes referred to as strA. Confers resistance to aminoglycoside class antimicrobials (Category II) that are not used in feedlot production medicine; often detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in complete linkage with APH(3')-Ia, aph(6)-Id, and sul2"
APH(6)-Id	"sometimes referred to as strB. Confers resistance to aminoglycoside class antimicrobials (Category II) that are not used in feedlot production medicine; often detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in complete linkage with APH(3')-Ia, APH(3'')-Ib, and sul2"

aadA25	"confers resistance to aminoglycoside (Category II) and aminocyclitol (Category III) class antimicrobials, neither of which are used in feedlot production medicine; occasionally detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in a resistance region with blaOXA-2, near to msrE-mphE and tetH"
aadA31	"confers resistance to aminoglycoside (Category II) and aminocyclitol (Category III) class antimicrobials, neither of which are used in feedlot production medicine; has been detected on integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) in a resistance region near tetH in P. multocida and H. somni isolates"
blaROB1	"confers resistance to penicillin class antimicrobials (including ampicillin and penicillin, Category II), but not cephalosporins (including ceftiofur, Category I); has been detected in both integrative and conjugative elements (ICEs) and plasmids in BRD-associated bacteria"
